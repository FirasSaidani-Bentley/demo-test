# Value

## What changes

A dimension change that previously required a CAD redraw (30–90 minutes) now takes 3 seconds. The architect changes a slider — width, depth, height, roof pitch — and the 3D model updates live with annotations showing the new values directly on the geometry. Clients can validate on the spot. Fewer revision cycles. Fewer miscommunications.

## Measurable impact

- **Revision cycles reduced**: Target 50% fewer back-and-forth rounds on dimension sign-off.
- **Time to client validation**: From days (redraw + re-send) to minutes (live session).
- **Demo engagement**: A 3D interactive model holds attention in a client meeting; a PDF does not. Measurable via adoption at client-facing sessions.
- **Prototype metric**: Load in under 2 seconds, dimension update in under 100ms, works on a standard laptop with no install.

## Bentley alignment

Bentley's core value proposition is reducing the cost of building and operating infrastructure. A parametric 3D viewer directly reduces rework cost in the design phase. It demonstrates Bentley's commitment to accessible visualization beyond expert tools, and complements the iTwin platform narrative by showing that connected, interactive models can start simple and grow in complexity.
